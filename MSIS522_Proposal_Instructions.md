MSIS 522 Project Proposal — Instructions

Overview
Building on your experience from MSIS 549, the MSIS 522 project focuses on applying generative AI technologies to create an innovative solution to a clear problem/pain point. Your proposal should demonstrate how you'll leverage generative AI to address meaningful business or societal challenges. You can build on one of the teammates' MSIS 549 projects or choose something new; it is up to you. We'll have a new set of judges :) The proposal is graded only on completion (Pass/Fail).

Submission Requirements
Create a concise proposal document (1-2 pages) that includes:

Team Information
Names and emails of all team members
Elevator Pitch
Write a short paragraph that clearly details the project scope. For instance, you can structure the pitch of your product in this format:
"A lot of [target customers] struggle with [pain point], which costs them [consequence]. So we built [product], a [simple category description] that helps them [clear result] by [simple mechanism]. Unlike [competitor or outdated method], we [key differentiator]."
Project Description
If building on your MSIS 522 project:
Summarize your previous project
Clearly explain what you're updating, changing, or improving
If proposing a new project:
Problem statement and its significance
How AI technologies address this problem
Data sources and anticipated challenges
Implementation Plan
Specific generative AI models or technologies you plan to use
Technical approach and methodology
Demo concept explanation (what will you showcase in your final presentation?)
Final Presentation Guidelines
Time Limit: 5-6 minutes (exact time TBD)
Presentation Structure (subject to changes):
2-3 team members presenting
Remaining members responsible for Q&A with judges
Must include a live demo of your tool
Prepare a backup recording in case of technical difficulties
Focus Areas (prioritize these elements due to the short presentation time):
Story and problem statement. A clear problem should be of the highest importance!
Your solution approach
Live demonstration
Highlight ONE or TWO key strengths of your product (choose the most compelling):
Business model viability
Technical innovation
Scalability/growth potential
Competitive advantage
Social impact/value
Whatever you think is most relevant for you
